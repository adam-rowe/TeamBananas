About



+ What is our site?

Our site is a place where potential or current students can ask question about certain colleges or subjects and get their questions answered from students that have been through what you've asked!


+ Where did we get our idea?

All of us are students and when we were deciding on our college we looked the college and all we could find was biased content from the college's marketing department, so we wanted to create a site were people can ask the student of the college themselves.



Our site is an online forum that allows students to ask and answer question they may have relating to their college.



Join the discussion and enter our live chat, a live chatroom where you can ask question and get them answered by anyone thats online.
